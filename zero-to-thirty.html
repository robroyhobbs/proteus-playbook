Engagement Plan

First 30 days: audit to working system

Ship early. Segment-first. System over campaigns. Visible outputs by week 2.

Week 1 · Days 1-5

Discovery & Proof Inventory

  • Audit all content: site, social, testimonials, video, founder appearances
  • Interview Sam + Caley: what proof exists, what's missing, what works in sales
  • Map 3 wedges: who buys, why, what proof moves them
  • Assess website messaging against each segment
Week 2 · Days 6-12

Messaging & First Assets

  • Segment messaging framework: headline, value prop, proof stack, objection handling per wedge
  • Atomize strongest proof source into 8-12 assets
  • Draft website recommendations for top-priority segment
  • Set up AI content workflow with Caley
Week 3 · Days 13-20

Content System & Founder Voice

  • Founder content sequence: 3-5 posts from Sam's POV
  • Walk Caley through AI workflow
  • First segment landing page copy
  • Atomize second proof source for different segment
Week 4 · Days 21-30

Review & Roadmap

  • Review performance signals from published content
  • 90-day content and growth roadmap by segment
  • Prioritize: which wedge to double down, what proof to capture next
  • Define ongoing engagement based on what we've learned

Experiments

What we build and test in the first 30 days

Not just strategy — specific lead gen assets shipped, measured, and iterated.

Website

Segment landing pages

  • Build: 3 dedicated pages — /baseball, /longevity, /elite — with segment-specific hero, proof, and CTA
  • Test: Route paid/organic traffic by segment and compare conversion rate vs. generic homepage
  • Ship by: Week 2
Lead Magnets

Segment-specific downloads

  • "Baseball Trainer's Guide to 3D Assessment" — protocols, data screenshots, what traditional tools miss
  • "Healthspan Benchmarks Report" — functional capacity by age, trained vs. untrained
  • "Advanced 3D Protocols" — real protocols from Stroupe, Cressey, Walsh (elite)
  • Ship by: Week 2 (baseball), Week 3 (longevity + elite)
Email Nurture

Segment-tagged sequences

  • Baseball: 4-email proof-driven sequence ending in 15-min walkthrough CTA
  • Longevity: 5-email clinical confidence sequence (longer nurture for this buyer)
  • Elite: 4-email peer proof sequence ending in peer introduction
  • Ship by: Week 3
Video

Programmatic short-form clips

  • Remotion templates: Stat card, testimonial overlay, data visualization — reusable across segments
  • First batch: 10-15 clips from existing footage, segmented by audience and platform (9:16, 1:1, 16:9)
  • Demo reel: 60-second "Proteus in action" per segment — what the data looks like in real time
  • Ship by: Week 3
Founder Voice

Sam's LinkedIn presence

  • 3-5 posts: POV on 3D resistance, why current tools miss movement quality, what Proteus sees that others can't
  • 1 long-form piece: "Why we built Proteus" — founder story that establishes category credibility
  • Test: Measure engagement rate vs. brand account posts
  • Ship by: Week 2-3 (rolling)
Proof Library

Organized, reusable proof assets

  • Testimonial cards: Quote + name + role + outcome — formatted for social, email, and sales
  • Case study pages: 1 flagship per segment (from atomization work)
  • Stat one-liners: Proof snippets for sales team to drop into conversations
  • Ship by: Week 2 (first segment), Week 4 (all three)

The experiment mindset: We don't need to guess which approach works. We ship all six in 30 days, measure what moves pipeline, and double down on winners in the 90-day roadmap. The AI workflow makes this possible — without it, this would take a 4-person team 3 months.

How we know it's working

Leading (Days 1-30)

  • Content published per week (4-6x current)
  • Segment messaging live on site
  • Founder content engagement rate
  • AI workflow adopted

Lagging (Days 30-90)

  • Inbound inquiry volume by segment
  • Traffic to segment-specific pages
  • Content-attributed conversations
  • Community growth acceleration

Qualitative

  • "I saw your post about..." in sales calls
  • Trainers sharing content organically
  • Prospects self-identifying by segment
  • Sam's founder voice gaining traction

Visible outputs by week 2. Working system by week 4. After that, we decide together what ongoing engagement looks like.
