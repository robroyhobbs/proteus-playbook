Week-by-week detail

Wk 1
Discovery & Proof Inventory — audit content, interview Sam + Caley, map 3 segments
  • Audit all content: site, social, testimonials, video, founder appearances
  • Interview Sam + Caley: what proof exists, what's missing, what works in sales
  • Map 3 wedges: who buys, why, what proof moves them
  • Assess website messaging against each segment

Delivers: Proof inventory + segment map + audit findings

Wk 2
Messaging & First Assets — framework per segment, atomize first proof source
  • Segment messaging framework: headline, value prop, proof stack, objection handling per wedge
  • Atomize strongest proof source into 8-12 assets
  • Draft website recommendations for top-priority segment
  • Set up AI content workflow with Caley

Delivers: Messaging framework (3 segments) + first content pack (8-12 assets)

Wk 3
Content System & Founder Voice — Sam's posts, video batch, AI workflow handoff
  • Founder content sequence: 3-5 posts from Sam's POV
  • Walk Caley through AI workflow
  • First segment landing page copy
  • Atomize second proof source for different segment

Delivers: Founder content + landing page copy + second content pack + AI workflow handoff

Wk 4
Review & Roadmap — measure results, prioritize segments, plan next 90 days
  • Review performance signals from published content
  • 90-day content and growth roadmap by segment
  • Prioritize: which wedge to double down, what proof to capture next
  • Define ongoing engagement based on what we've learned

Delivers: Performance review + 90-day roadmap + engagement recommendation

Experiments

Six things we ship and test

Not strategy decks — real assets, measured and iterated. Click to expand.

Website
Segment Landing Pages Ship: Wk 2
  • Build: 3 dedicated pages — /baseball, /longevity, /elite — segment-specific hero, proof, and CTA
  • Test: Route traffic by segment, compare conversion vs. generic homepage
  • Tools: HTML/CSS or Next.js + Tailwind. v0 for prototyping. Claude for copy.
Lead Magnets
Segment-Specific Downloads Ship: Wk 2-3
  • "Baseball Trainer's Guide to 3D Assessment" — protocols, data screenshots, what traditional tools miss
  • "Healthspan Benchmarks Report" — functional capacity by age, trained vs. untrained
  • "Advanced 3D Protocols" — real protocols from Stroupe, Cressey, Walsh (elite)
  • Tools: Claude for content. Puppeteer/React-PDF for branded PDF. AI image gen for data viz.
Email
Nurture Sequences via HubSpot Ship: Wk 3
  • Baseball: 4-email proof sequence → 15-min walkthrough CTA
  • Longevity: 5-email clinical confidence sequence (longer nurture)
  • Elite: 4-email peer proof sequence → peer introduction
  • Tools: Claude for copy. HubSpot for delivery, workflows, segment tags, lead scoring.
Video
Programmatic Short-Form Clips Ship: Wk 3
  • Remotion templates: Stat card, testimonial overlay, data viz — reusable across segments
  • First batch: 10-15 clips from existing footage, per segment + platform (9:16, 1:1, 16:9)
  • Demo reel: 60-second "Proteus in action" per segment
  • Tools: Remotion + FFmpeg + AI for scripts. Caley's raw footage.
Founder
Sam's LinkedIn Presence Ship: Wk 2-3
  • 3-5 posts: POV on 3D resistance, what Proteus sees that others can't
  • 1 long-form: "Why we built Proteus" — founder story, category credibility
  • Test: Engagement rate vs. brand account posts
  • Tools: Claude for drafts. AI angle generation (10-15 → Sam picks 4-5).
Proof
Organized Proof Library Ship: Wk 2-4
  • Testimonial cards: Quote + name + role + outcome — social, email, sales formats
  • Case study pages: 1 flagship per segment
  • Stat one-liners: Proof snippets for sales conversations
  • Tools: Claude for extraction. HTML templates for proof cards. Atomizer for multi-format.

The experiment mindset: Ship all six in 30 days, measure what moves pipeline, double down on winners in the 90-day roadmap.
