Playbook / 0-30 Plan
Engagement Plan

First 30 days: audit to working system

Ship early. Segment-first. System over campaigns. Visible outputs by week 2.

Week 1 · Days 1-5

Discovery & Proof Inventory

  • Audit all content: site, social, testimonials, video, founder appearances
  • Interview Sam + Caley: what proof exists, what's missing, what works in sales
  • Map 3 wedges: who buys, why, what proof moves them
  • Assess website messaging against each segment
Week 2 · Days 6-12

Messaging & First Assets

  • Segment messaging framework: headline, value prop, proof stack, objection handling per wedge
  • Atomize strongest proof source into 8-12 assets
  • Draft website recommendations for top-priority segment
  • Set up AI content workflow with Caley
Week 3 · Days 13-20

Content System & Founder Voice

  • Founder content sequence: 3-5 posts from Sam's POV
  • Walk Caley through AI workflow
  • First segment landing page copy
  • Atomize second proof source for different segment
Week 4 · Days 21-30

Review & Roadmap

  • Review performance signals from published content
  • 90-day content and growth roadmap by segment
  • Prioritize: which wedge to double down, what proof to capture next
  • Define ongoing engagement based on what we've learned

Deliverables

Wk 1

Proof inventory + segment map + audit

Wk 2

Messaging framework (3 segments) + first content pack (8-12 assets)

Wk 3

Founder content + landing page copy + second content pack + AI workflow handoff

Wk 4

Performance review + 90-day roadmap + engagement recommendation

By day 14: Messaging framework + 8-12 new assets ready to publish. Not a strategy deck — real assets.

Working together

Matt

  • Messaging strategy & segment positioning
  • Content atomization & AI workflow
  • Website copy & landing pages
  • Founder content (Sam's voice)
  • Growth roadmap & prioritization

Caley (amplified)

  • Community & engagement
  • Visual content & shoots
  • Ambassador program & relationships
  • Platform execution & scheduling
  • Brand voice & quality control

By week 3, Caley has the AI workflow and messaging framework to produce segment-specific content at higher volume — with or without ongoing support. Build capability, not dependency.

How we know it's working

Leading (Days 1-30)

  • Content published per week (4-6x current)
  • Segment messaging live on site
  • Founder content engagement rate
  • AI workflow adopted

Lagging (Days 30-90)

  • Inbound inquiry volume by segment
  • Traffic to segment-specific pages
  • Content-attributed conversations
  • Community growth acceleration

Qualitative

  • "I saw your post about..." in sales calls
  • Trainers sharing content organically
  • Prospects self-identifying by segment
  • Sam's founder voice gaining traction

Visible outputs by week 2. Working system by week 4. After that, we decide together what ongoing engagement looks like.
