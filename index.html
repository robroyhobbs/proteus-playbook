Generic messaging

Fix: Proof library + softer CTAs by segment.

Social doesn't capture

20k+ IG, 10k TikTok — but content drives awareness, not leads. No value exchange.

Fix: Lead magnets by segment (guides, benchmarks, toolkits).

No segment-specific nurture

All leads get the same follow-up. Generic nurture = low conversion.

Fix: Segment-tagged capture → tailored email sequences.
