Messaging architecture

Primary: "Agency over aging"

Not training to win — training to maintain and improve physical capacity. Proteus gives the data to know if it's working.

Secondary: "Differentiate your practice"

Every longevity practice claims "evidence-based." Proteus makes it real. The 3D assessment becomes why high-value clients choose you.

Objection: "Is this for athletes?"

Biggest barrier: Proteus's brand leans athletic. Every visual, testimonial, and use case must show people like their clients.

Language shifts

Don't say:

  • Game changer
  • Athletic performance
  • Competitive edge

Do say:

  • Precision assessment
  • Functional capacity
  • Healthspan optimization

Where they discover

  • LinkedIn: "Your clients track everything except physical performance"
  • Podcasts: Longevity/healthspan shows (Attia-adjacent)
  • Google: "Functional strength assessment for aging"
  • Conferences: Functional medicine, longevity networks

Lead magnets

  • "Practitioner's Guide to 3D Movement Assessment" — protocol, data interpretation, client templates
  • "Healthspan Benchmarks" — functional capacity by age, trained vs. untrained
  • Webinar: "Add Objective Movement Assessment to Your Practice"

5-email nurture (longer for this buyer)

  • 1: Resource + "The metric that predicts fall risk better than questionnaires"
  • 2: Practitioner case study
  • 3: Client before/after — 58-year-old executive, 6 months of data
  • 4: Facility ROI — retention and referral impact
  • 5: Soft CTA — "15-min walkthrough of the assessment"

Conversion notes

  • Higher-touch for higher-ticket. More nurture than baseball.
  • Best CTA: "Visit a practice near you that uses Proteus"
  • Never discount. This audience equates price with quality.

Revenue signal: Highest-LTV segment. Affluent, retention-oriented, referral-driven. One successful facility becomes a case study that sells 10 more.
