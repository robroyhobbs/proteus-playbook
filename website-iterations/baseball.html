Messaging architecture

Primary: "Measure what you couldn't before"

They already track bat speed and exit velo. The gap is movement quality — how force is produced. Proteus fills it.

Secondary: "Prove your program works"

Parents and coaching staffs want evidence. 3D data becomes proof that differentiates the facility.

Objection: "Expensive toy?"

Every proof point must show: "We saw X, changed Y, result was Z." Data that changes decisions, not just looks cool.

CTA: "See how trainers use Proteus"

Not "Book a demo." This buyer wants to see peers first. Bridge to case studies and trainer spotlights.
