AI collapses production, not judgment

One person producing at the scale of a small team. 1 interview → 15-20 calibrated outputs in 2-3 days.

Human stays in control: Source capture, angle selection, voice calibration, strategy. The judgment calls AI can't make.

AI accelerates: Transcription, proof extraction, drafts, platform adaptation, scheduling. The 80% of time that's production.

What changes

Without AI

  • 1 interview → 2-3 assets/week
  • 80% production, 20% strategy
  • Same angle, all platforms
  • One segment per cycle

With AI workflow

  • 1 interview → 15-20 assets in 2-3 days
  • 60% strategy, 40% production
  • Platform-native adaptation per channel
  • 3 segments served simultaneously

Video & visual production at scale

The content engine isn't just text. Programmatic video tools extend the same workflow to visual assets.

Remotion

Programmatic video

React-based video generation. Create templates once, then produce segment-specific variants automatically: stat cards, testimonial clips, data visualizations, product demos — all from the same source data.

Batch production

One template → dozens of clips

Design a "rotational power stat" video template. Feed in different athlete data, trainer quotes, or segment messaging → get 15-20 unique short-form clips without manual editing.

Platform-native

Auto-format per channel

Same content, different specs: 9:16 for Reels/TikTok, 1:1 for feed, 16:9 for YouTube. Text overlays for silent autoplay. Branded consistently across all outputs.

Applied to Proteus: One quarterly shoot with Caley's team becomes the raw footage. Remotion templates turn that into segment-specific video assets — baseball trainer clips get rotational power data overlays, longevity clips get healthspan messaging, elite clips get peer testimonial formats. Same footage, 3x the output.
