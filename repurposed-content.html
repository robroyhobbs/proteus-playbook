The source

Boltline (manufacturing traceability) + ispace (lunar lander company). One case study: 99.9%+ inventory accuracy across 3 continents on multimillion-dollar flight hardware.

What was extracted

  • Contrarian: "The lunar lander company trusts this startup"
  • Data: 99.9%+ accuracy, 3 continents
  • Status: ispace as a credibility anchor
  • Operational: Real workflow details for similar buyers

Platform adaptation

LinkedIn

Authority angle. Contrarian insight, long-form.

X

Hook-first threads. Data-forward, shareable.

Website

Trust-building. Structured case study, SEO.

Email

Outcome-led. Short narrative, bridges to CTA.

Sales

Proof snippets. Quote cards, stat one-liners.

Video

15-60s clips. Text overlay for silent play.

Blog

Deep-dive. Canonical reference, backlinks.

Community

Discussion starters. Question-led engagement.
